Developer: Fancy Fish Games

Witherspring Wilds is an open world life sim. Build a village, tame wild creatures, and explore dangerous lands. Farm, fight, craft, and befriend NPCs — how will you shape the Wilds?

An expansive open-world experience where every choice shapes the world around you. Witherspring Wilds blends the cosy familiarity of life sims with the thrill of adventure and discovery.

  • Build — Construct and grow your own village from the ground up.
  • Befriend — Form bonds with a cast of unique NPCs, each with their own stories.
  • Explore — Venture into dangerous, uncharted lands filled with secrets.
  • Craft — Combine materials to create tools, gear, and more.
  • Fight — Battle the creatures and forces that threaten the Wilds.
  • Tame — Earn the trust of wild creatures and make them your companions.
  • Farm — Cultivate the land to sustain your growing village.
  • Mine — Delve underground to uncover rare resources and hidden wonders.
