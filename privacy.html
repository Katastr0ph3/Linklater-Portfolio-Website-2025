Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who I am

This website is operated by Cat Linklater, a freelance game audio professional based in Aberdeen, UK. You can contact me at info@catlinklateraudio.com.

What data I collect and why

Contact form. When you fill in the contact form on this site, your name, email address, and message are sent directly to my email inbox. I use this information only to respond to your enquiry. I do not store it in any database, share it with third parties, or use it for marketing.

Email correspondence. If you email me directly, I'll keep a record of that conversation in my inbox in order to follow up. I won't share it with anyone else.

No analytics. This site does not currently use Google Analytics or any other tracking service. I do not collect data about how you browse the site.

Third-party embeds

This site embeds videos from Vimeo. When a Vimeo video loads, Vimeo may set cookies on your device and collect data about your visit in accordance with Vimeo's own privacy policy. I have no control over this data collection.

Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. The only cookies that may be set come from the Vimeo video embeds described above.

Your rights under UK GDPR

If you have contacted me through this site, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data I hold about you
  • Ask me to correct or delete it
  • Withdraw consent for me to keep it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email me at info@catlinklateraudio.com and I'll respond within 30 days.

If you have a complaint about how I've handled your data, you can contact the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Changes to this policy

If anything significant changes (for example, if I add analytics), I'll update this page. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.

Interested in collaborating?

If you would like creative assistance on video game or film projects then feel free to get in touch. I'd love to have a conversation with you about your project.
