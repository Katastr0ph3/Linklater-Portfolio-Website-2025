Hi, I'm Cat

I'm a passionate sound designer and composer who thrives on crafting immersive sonic experiences. I blend elements from diverse genres to evoke emotion and enhance storytelling through audio. As a versatile recording artist, I'm skilled in field recording, studio engineering, violin performance, and vocals — and I have a love for collecting unique instruments and sound-producing objects to broaden my creative palette.

Education

MFA in Sound Design & Composition for Video Games from the University of Chichester (UK) · BMus from Rhodes University (South Africa).

How much will it cost?

It takes 30 minutes to work that out.

Sound for games isn't one-size-fits-all. Your game has its own feel, scope, and needs — and the quote should reflect that, not a flat rate off a price list.

To give you an accurate price, I start with a free 30–60 minute Discovery Call. I'll ask about your project, your vision, and what you're hoping the audio will do for players. From there, I put together a tailored proposal.

This call also helps us figure out if we're a good match. I care about the projects I take on, and I want to make sure I can genuinely make your game sound great.

Get started

  1. Send me an email at info@catlinklateraudio.com
  2. I'll send you a link to book a free 30–60 minute Discovery Call.
