About me

I am a passionate sound designer and composer who thrives on crafting immersive sonic experiences. I believe in the boundless possibilities of blending elements from diverse genres and human timbres, constantly discovering new ways to evoke emotion and enhance storytelling through audio. As a versatile recording artist, I am skilled in field recording, studio engineering, violin performance, and vocals. I have a love for collecting unique instruments and sound-producing objects to broaden my creative palette. Education

I hold a Bachelor of Music (BMus) degree from Rhodes University in South Africa, where I developed a solid foundation in music theory, performance, audio mixing and composition. Building on this, I pursued a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Sound Design and Composition for Video Games at the University of Chichester in the UK. This unique dual-focus program honed my skills in crafting immersive audio experiences and composing dynamic, impactful soundtracks tailored for interactive media.
